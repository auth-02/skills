Trajectory · distance from the Sun

One probe, a little faster.

Voyager 1 took the aggressive slingshot past Saturn, bending up out of the planetary plane. Voyager 2 stayed for the full tour — and trails its twin by about 25 AU.

VOYAGER // DECK
03 / 06
The grand tour · a once-in-176-years alignment

Each planet flung it onward.

  • 1979 · Jupiter — active volcanoes on Io, the first seen beyond Earth.
  • 1980 · Saturn — Voyager 1 dives past Titan and leaves the planetary plane.
  • 1986 · Uranus — Voyager 2 alone; the only craft ever to visit.
  • 1989 · Neptune — the last planet, and the end of the tour.
  • 2012 / 2018 · Interstellar — both cross the heliopause, six years apart.
